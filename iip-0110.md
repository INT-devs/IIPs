# IIP-110: Timewarp Mitigation
## Abstract
Documents INTcoin's adoption of BIP-94 timewarp attack mitigation. Constrains the relationship between consecutive block timestamps to prevent miners from manipulating difficulty through timestamp games. Applied on all networks.
## Specification
Implements [BIP-94](https://github.com/bitcoin/bips/blob/master/bip-0094.mediawiki) without divergence. Block templates account for the timewarp rule when computing the maximum allowed timestamp.
