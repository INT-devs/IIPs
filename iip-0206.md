# IIP-206: Fee Filter
## Abstract
Documents INTcoin's adoption of BIP-133 FEEFILTER message, allowing peers to communicate the minimum fee rate they will accept for transaction relay. Implemented without modification.
## Specification
Implements [BIP-133](https://github.com/bitcoin/bips/blob/master/bip-0133.mediawiki) without divergence.
