# IIP-111: CPFP Topology Restrictions
## Abstract
Documents INTcoin's adoption of BIP-431 topology restrictions for Child-Pays-For-Parent (CPFP) transaction clusters in the mempool. Limits cluster sizes to bound the computational cost of block assembly and fee evaluation.
## Specification
Implements [BIP-431](https://github.com/bitcoin/bips/blob/master/bip-0431.mediawiki) without divergence.
