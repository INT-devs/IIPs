# IIP-207: WTXID Relay
## Abstract
Documents INTcoin's adoption of BIP-339 WTXIDRELAY for announcing transactions by witness txid. Implemented without modification.
## Specification
Implements [BIP-339](https://github.com/bitcoin/bips/blob/master/bip-0339.mediawiki) without divergence. Peers signal WTXIDRELAY support during VERSION handshake.
