# IIP-205: Header Announcements
## Abstract
Documents INTcoin's adoption of BIP-130 SENDHEADERS for direct header-based block announcements instead of inv-based announcements. Implemented without modification.
## Specification
Implements [BIP-130](https://github.com/bitcoin/bips/blob/master/bip-0130.mediawiki) without divergence. Peers exchange SENDHEADERS during handshake to opt into headers-first announcements.
