# IIP-412: Timelock Recovery
## Abstract
Documents INTcoin's adoption of BIP-128 timelock-based social recovery. Allows users to configure a time-delayed recovery path with guardian signatures, providing a safety net against key loss without compromising day-to-day security. Adapted for Dilithium5 signatures.
## Specification
Implements [BIP-128](https://github.com/bitcoin/bips/blob/master/bip-0128.mediawiki) with Dilithium5 (IIP-0300) guardian signatures. Recovery requires threshold guardian approval after a configurable timelock period.
### RPC commands
- `recovery_create`, `recovery_info`, `recovery_trigger`, `recovery_cancel`, `recovery_execute`, `recovery_status`, `recovery_list`
