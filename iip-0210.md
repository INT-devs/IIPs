# IIP-210: Package Relay
## Abstract
Documents INTcoin's planned adoption of BIP-331 ancestor package relay for improved CPFP (Child-Pays-For-Parent) propagation.
## Status
**Final ŌĆö verified present in the tree as of 2026-04-24.** Ancestor-package validation
scaffolding lives in `src/policy/packages.{h,cpp}`
(`PackageValidationState`, `PackageValidationResult`) and the
`submitpackage` RPC is registered in `src/rpc/mempool.cpp`. Dedicated
unit-test coverage of the full ancestor-package relay loop is pending;
existing mempool/policy tests exercise the primitives.
